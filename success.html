How to be successful

We’re looking for kids who are good on camera — and parents ready to support them.

Good on camera means

  • Being articulate
  • Feeling natural
  • Being themselves

Kids should also have a basic business understanding and show a willingness to learn, take feedback, and put in high effort.

Parent support is critical

Demonstrating real parent involvement is one of the biggest things we look for. Show us you’re ready to help film and cheer them on.
