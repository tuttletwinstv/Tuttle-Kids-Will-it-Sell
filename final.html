Tuttle Twins Cartoon Entrepreneur Series

A new unscripted live-action series that follows kids as they pitch, build, and launch a real business in the lead-up to a kids’ entrepreneur market in Lindon, Utah.

Selected participants work with two coaches, film at home, and present their business on camera.

Compensation: $500 and a booth at the Lindon Kids Market.

Apply

How it works

  • Submit an application with a few short videos.
  • If selected, a team member makes three visits to your home to film between July 20 and August 8.
  • Kids and parents film and upload additional video from a phone.

Pitch submissions

Three short videos, uploaded on the application form.

  1. Business pitch Max 30 seconds

    Describe the product or service you would create and sell. Include your name, one fact about you, and your city.

  2. Introduction Max 60 seconds, unedited

    Tell us who you are and why you should be selected for the series.

  3. Parent / guardian intro Max 30 seconds, unedited

    A parent confirms they will participate, help film, and support their child.
