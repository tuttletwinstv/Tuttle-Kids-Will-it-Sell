FAQs

Do I have to apply for the Lindon Kids Market on August 8?
No. Kids selected for the series will automatically get a booth slot.
Can multiple kids from the same family apply?
Yes! However, you must apply together in one single audition.
I’ll be out of town for part of the time. Can I still apply?
Yes, but please indicate your availability on the application form.
Do I need to have attended a kids market or fair before?
No. Whether you’re a seasoned veteran or trying something new, you are welcome to apply.
I already sell a product or run a business. Can I use that?
Yes, but please include in your pitch video how you hope to enhance your business.
